Salgirah Festival

Branding, Motion, Environmental

Client: The Ismaili Community

Salgirah is a festival observed by Ismaili community marking the birthday of their spiritual leader. His Highness the Aga Khan V was appointed the role of Imam to 15 million Ismailis worldwide in 2025. October 12 marked his first Salgirah.

In 2025, the identity launched to over 1 million social media impressions across 21 countries in 11 languages. The global activation demonstrates how a unified identity travels across unexpected materials and age groups.
