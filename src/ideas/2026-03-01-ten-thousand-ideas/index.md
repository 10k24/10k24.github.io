## Ten Thousand Ideas


Before AI, might hoard notebooks or Trello boards of ideas hoping one day we might get to execute on one of them. Maybe one of those ideas could make us rich.

Now, in the age of genAI, we can produce amounts of anything: documents, images, videos, code, entire functional websites, and more. The cost of maintaining an idea has grown exponentially, not in terms of cost but mental bandwidth.

What was once an email or a few bullet points is now hundreds of lines of code, several open browser tabs, and as many chat windows with various AI services.

In any moment we can only truly focus on one thing, so what do we do when one thought now carries a day’s worth of context?
